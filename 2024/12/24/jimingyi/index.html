Jimingyi - the Ancient Post Station
Dec 24, 2024

鸡鸣驿位于河北省怀来县，是一座古邮驿。如今，鸡鸣驿南侧的空地上建起了现代的风力发电设施，与古城交相辉映。

Jimingyi is an ancient post station located in Huailai County, Hebei Province. Jiming is its name, which literally means rooster crowing, while yi means post station. Nowadays, modern wind turbines are built south of the ancient post station, creating a scene in which modernity and antiquity coexists in perfect harmony.
