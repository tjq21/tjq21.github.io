Fort Lauderdale Tri-Rail Station
Mar 27, 2026

劳德代尔堡站。位于佛罗里达州劳德代尔堡市。

大巴停在公路对面的巨大露天 P+R 停车场。从停车场走向车站，棕红色屋顶的站房出现在水泥和沥青的灰色世界中。

作为“阳光之州”佛罗里达州南部的一座车站，蓝天、白云、棕榈树和棕红色的瓦给这座车站带来了不少南国风情，让人想起日本千叶县的馆山站。车站后方是繁忙的95号州际公路。此站由迈阿密都市圈通勤铁路Tri-Rail与美铁（Amtrak）共享。

Fort Lauderdale Station, a station at Fort Lauderdale, FL.

The bus stopped at the huge Park & Ride across the road. Walking toward the station, the building emerged into view - its red-tiled roof standing out against the gray landscape of concrete and asphalt.

With blue sky, white clouds, palm trees, and red-tiled roof, the station in Southern Florida, the Sunshine State, brings its passengers abundant tropical charm, which reminds me of Tateyama Station in Chiba Prefecture, Japan. Behind the station is the busy Interstate I-95. The station is shared by Miami metropolitan area commuter rail Tri-Rail and Amtrak.
