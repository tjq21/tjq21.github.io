Yanji Intercity Bus Station
May 2, 2023

晚上的延吉汽车站。延吉位于延边朝鲜族自治州，可以看到汽车站有中文（左）和朝鲜语（右）双语标识。汽车站右侧是铁路延吉西站，面前的是延吉公交的延吉西站场站。这附近除了建筑物和一条通向市区的公路之外，只有黑暗。

Yanji Intercity Bus Station at night. Yanji is a city in Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture in northeastern China, so you can see the Bus Station marked in both Chinese (left) and Korean (right). On the right side of the Bus Station is China Railway’s Yanjixi (Yanji West) Station. At the very front of the photo is the parking lot for the municipal intracity bus. There was only darkness except for the buildings and a highway to downtown.
