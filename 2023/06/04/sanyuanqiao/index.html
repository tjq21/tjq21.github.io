A Common Summer Day in Beijing
Jun 4, 2023

夏天的北京三元桥附近，从机场高速上方的天桥向二环方向望去。笔者刚从首都机场（PEK）附近乘公共汽车经机场高速到这里，从画面右下角的车站下车。这站原名三元桥，后因距离三元桥太远，为防止误导更名左家庄桥东。

A summer scene near Sanyuanqiao, Beijing, on a pedestrian overpass over Airport Expressway. I had just taken a bus here from somewhere near Capital Airport (PEK), via Airport Expressway. The bus stop where I got off can be seen at the bottom-right of the picture. Its name was initially Sanyuanqiao, but since the bus stop is too far away from Sanyuanqiao Bridge, its name was recently changed into Zuojiazhuang Qiao Dong (Zuojiazhuang Bridge East) to avoid misleading.
