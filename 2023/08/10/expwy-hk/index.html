On a Double-Decker Bus in Hong Kong
Aug 10, 2023

这张照片拍摄于香港的55路巴士的二楼。巴士正运行在三号干线葵涌附近，画面左侧是货柜码头。我搭此巴士前往屯门，随后转车前往港珠澳大桥。

This picture was taken on the second floor of a Bus 55 of Hong Kong, which was running on Route 3 near Kwai Chung. On the left side of the picture is a container terminal. I rode this bus to go to Tuen Mun, then change another bus to visit Hong Kong-Zhuhai-Macau Bridge (HZMB).
